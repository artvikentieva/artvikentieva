About me

Natalia Vikentyeva was born in 1974 in Ukraine, lives and
works in Kyiv. Since childhood - with a pencil and paints.
Studied in different art studios.
Easily works with oil, acrylic, graphics, sculpture and
all interior decorative materials.
Constant experimentation with materials, optimism and
work as an artist decorator formed their
own style of the artist Natalia Vikentyeva.
- "What's inside, so outside! Need to live in an environment
positive images and colors!” the artist is convinced.
Her paintings are a fabulous world of realization of the viewer's fantasies about omnipotence, courage,
freedom of choice and freedom of movement. A mixture of different techniques and materials reveal
many solutions for the fantasies and freedom of thought of the viewer. Thus it seems
the idea of ​​the joy of life, beauty, freedom and love, manifested at the same time by tradition and
timeliness of painting.
At the moment, Natalia is working on her series of paintings "Quarantine Yoga","
The secret of happiness", " Windows to the sky. Prepares to participate in exhibitions and competitions.
Contests: 1st place All-Ukrainian competition " Literary visa-free"
Publications: 2019 All-World Literature 2006 Healthy Life Guide Exhibitions: 24.12.21 Globus Gallery
